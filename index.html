Family pilot

You run the household. This is your help desk.

FamCEO gives your family one phone number. Everyone texts what they need — a reminder, a request, a permission slip, "what's for dinner?" — and you triage it all from one place, with every message getting an answer. You're the CEO; FamCEO is the assistant that makes sure nothing falls through.

How it works

1
A family member texts the house number with whatever they need.
2
FamCEO organizes it for you and instantly texts back an acknowledgment.
3
When you act on it, FamCEO texts the outcome — so every message gets a real answer.

Messaging & consent

FamCEO sends and receives SMS/MMS messages only between the account holder (a parent) and that parent's own family members.

  • How you're added: the account-holding parent registers each family member's mobile number with that member's consent. Numbers are never obtained from third parties or purchased lists.
  • What we send: message acknowledgments, chore reminders, shopping and dinner confirmations, and event reminders.
  • How often: message frequency varies with family activity, typically well under 500 messages per month.
  • Opting out: reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe; reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

Example messages: “FamCEO: Got it — the boss will see this and get back to you. Reply STOP to opt out.”  •  “FamCEO: Reminder — trash goes out tonight at 7pm. Reply DONE when it's done. Reply STOP to opt out.”

Privacy

Family messages are private. We collect the minimum needed to run the service — a name and a mobile number — and nothing more. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train anyone's models. A family can request full deletion of its data at any time.
